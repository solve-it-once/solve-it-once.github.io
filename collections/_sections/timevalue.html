On so1ve projects, you're buying the value of the requested website or improvements. Yes, we keep timesheets accurate to 15 minutes, but… hope we can agree to the honor system.

That is to say: It's equally likely we'll spend 49 or 51 hours in a sprint delivering what we said we would. so1ve promises to work that extra hour when necessary, and request in return that you don't demand every last minute.
